Cleaning Checklist

Guest-Ready Details Hosts Can Count On

This checklist gives hosts a clear idea of what we look for during a standard Airbnb or short-term rental turnover. Final services may vary by property, service package, and approved add-ons.

Turnover Standard

Our Guest-Ready Cleaning Focus

Every property is different, but our goal is always the same: clean, calm, stocked, and ready for the next guest.

🍽️

Kitchen

  • Wipe counters and surfaces
  • Clean sink and faucet area
  • Wipe appliance exteriors
  • Clean microwave when applicable
  • Check refrigerator condition
  • Remove trash and replace liners
  • Check dishes or dishwasher when instructed
🛁

Bathrooms

  • Clean and sanitize toilets
  • Clean sinks and counters
  • Clean mirrors
  • Clean showers and tubs
  • Replace towels when included
  • Restock toilet paper when supplied
  • Remove trash and reset area
🛏️

Bedrooms

  • Make beds when linens are available
  • Dust visible surfaces
  • Check under beds when needed
  • Reset pillows and blankets
  • Remove guest trash or personal items
  • Vacuum or sweep floors
  • Report visible damage when noticed
🛋️

Living Areas

  • Dust tables and visible surfaces
  • Reset couch pillows and throws
  • Vacuum rugs and floors
  • Wipe high-touch surfaces
  • Remove trash or left-behind items
  • Check general presentation
  • Prepare space for guest arrival
🧹

Floors

  • Sweep hard floors
  • Vacuum carpet and rugs
  • Mop approved flooring areas
  • Spot-check high-traffic areas
  • Check stairs when applicable
  • Remove visible debris
  • Report unusual stains or damage
🛒

Restocking

  • Toilet paper
  • Paper towels
  • Hand soap
  • Shampoo or toiletries
  • Coffee station supplies
  • Trash bags
  • Host-provided guest items
🧺

Laundry Support

  • Sheets when included
  • Towels when included
  • Bed reset support
  • Linen organization
  • Host-specific laundry instructions
  • Extra laundry may affect pricing
  • Off-site laundry may require approval
📸

Final Walkthrough

  • Check main guest areas
  • Confirm trash is removed
  • Look for visible damage
  • Check supplies when requested
  • Take photos if included
  • Report urgent concerns
  • Confirm guest-ready presentation
🎁

Special Setups

  • Welcome basket placement
  • Birthday decorations
  • Anniversary setup
  • Holiday touches
  • Romantic stay setup
  • Host-provided items only unless approved
  • Custom pricing may apply

Important Note

Checklist items may change based on your property and package.

Some tasks may require add-on pricing, extra time, special supplies, or prior approval. Excessive mess, heavy laundry, pet hair, unsafe conditions, biohazards, or unusual property conditions may require a revised quote.
