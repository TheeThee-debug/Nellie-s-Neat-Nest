Terms & Policies

Clear Expectations Protect Everyone

These policies help Nellie's Neat Nest LLC provide reliable service while protecting clients, properties, team members, and the business.

1. Service Estimates

All prices listed online are starting prices only. Final pricing may vary based on property size, condition, location, service type, laundry needs, restocking, special requests, and time required to complete the job properly.

2. Scheduling

Appointments are scheduled based on availability. Airbnb turnovers and short-term rental cleanings should be booked as early as possible, especially during weekends, holidays, and peak travel seasons.

3. Arrival Windows

Arrival windows may be affected by traffic, weather, previous appointments, property access, guest delays, or unexpected cleaning conditions. We will make reasonable efforts to communicate delays when they occur.

4. Payment Terms

Payment is due according to the approved quote, invoice, or written agreement. Large projects, first-time clients, emergency jobs, or special requests may require a deposit before service begins.

5. Cancellations

Please provide at least 24 hours notice when canceling or rescheduling whenever possible. Last-minute cancellations, lockouts, denied access, or same-day changes may be subject to a cancellation or trip fee.

6. Property Access

Clients are responsible for providing safe, accurate, and working access to the property. This includes door codes, lockbox instructions, parking directions, gate codes, and any special entry requirements.

7. Utilities

Water, electricity, lighting, heating/cooling when needed, and working plumbing must be available for cleaning services. If utilities are unavailable, service may be delayed, rescheduled, limited, or canceled.

8. Property Condition

Standard pricing assumes reasonable property conditions. Excessive trash, heavy grease, large amounts of pet hair, bodily fluids, biohazards, pest infestations, hoarding conditions, hazardous materials, or unsafe environments may require additional charges or refusal of service.

9. Valuables and Personal Items

Clients are responsible for securing cash, jewelry, firearms, medications, confidential documents, fragile valuables, and personal belongings before service. Nellie's Neat Nest LLC is not responsible for unsecured valuables or items not disclosed before service.

10. Damage and Pre-Existing Conditions

Nellie's Neat Nest LLC is not responsible for pre-existing damage, hidden defects, normal wear and tear, faulty fixtures, loose hardware, improperly installed items, manufacturer defects, weak surfaces, or damage caused by age, neglect, or prior use.

11. Damage Reporting

If visible damage or unusual property conditions are observed, we may document and report them to the client. Photo documentation may be used for quality control, service verification, or damage reporting when appropriate.

12. Photos and Documentation

With client permission or as part of an approved service, photos may be taken for documentation, quality control, before-and-after comparisons, restocking notes, or guest-ready confirmation. Customer privacy will be respected.

13. Restocking Services

Restocking services apply to supplies provided by the client unless otherwise agreed. Nellie's Neat Nest LLC is not responsible for missing supplies if the host has not provided adequate inventory before service.

14. Laundry Services

Laundry services may require additional time and fees. Excessive laundry, damaged linens, stained items, missing supplies, off-site laundry needs, or urgent timelines may affect pricing and availability.

15. Special Occasion Setups

Welcome baskets, decorations, romantic setups, birthday setups, holiday touches, and other custom requests must be approved in advance. Pricing may vary based on time, materials, complexity, and whether items are host-provided.

16. Pets

Pets must be disclosed before service. Pet hair, odors, stains, accidents, or pet-related messes may require additional time and charges.

17. Satisfaction Policy

If a client believes an agreed cleaning task was missed, the client should notify us within 24 hours when possible. We will make reasonable efforts to review and address legitimate concerns.

18. Refusal of Service

Nellie's Neat Nest LLC reserves the right to refuse, pause, or discontinue service due to unsafe conditions, harassment, nonpayment, biohazards, pests, illegal activity, severe property conditions, or any situation that may place team members at risk.

19. Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Nellie's Neat Nest LLC shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages. Liability for any approved claim shall be limited to the amount paid for the specific service giving rise to the claim, unless otherwise required by law.

20. Policy Updates

These terms and policies may be updated as the business grows. Continued use of services after updates means the client accepts the current version of the policies.
